App Support Center

Overview

Dial Indicator Helper is an offline dial-indicator utility. It does not require an account, does not include ads, and does not transmit your inputs or readings to any server.

Key Facts (for App Review)

  • Offline only: no cloud sync and no server connections are required for core features.
  • No data collection: the app does not collect personal information, identifiers, or usage analytics for the developer.
  • No tracking: the app does not use advertising or tracking SDKs.
  • Local storage: saved records (if any) are stored locally on your device.

FAQ

  • Does the app need the internet? No. The app works offline.
  • Does the app collect data? No. The app does not collect or transmit your personal data.
  • Does the app store anything? Saved records are stored locally on your device for your convenience.
